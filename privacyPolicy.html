Meem Privacy Policy

Meem [LLC] (“Meem”, “We”, or “Us”) is an app development company that owns and operates the application meem; a social networking service that allows users to engage in real conversations with one another (the “App”).

At Meem, We want to ensure that you are familiar with the ways that We may collect, use and, in some instances, disclose the data collected from you when you use our App, website, features, technologies and software (collectively, the “Services”).

What information do We collect? And how do We use it?

Meem collects different types of information from you as a user, such as your username, email address, how frequently you are are online, App performance logs. This information is used by Meem for different reasons, but in all cases, it may only be used for commercial purposes and in no other context. For example, some information is sometimes collected to provide, maintain, improve and customize our services. Other information is collected to be able to communicate with you. Information may also be collected to ensure your security.

To be more specific, Meem gathers information that falls under one of the following categories:

  • 1. Information that you provide Us with:

    This includes information that you, as a user, directly input into the App. This can occur in different situations:

    (A) when you sign up

    Meem requires a number of details from you when you sign up, in particular: your first name, last name, gender, date of birth, username and email address. Meem collects the above information to create users’ accounts, and to consequently, authenticate such accounts when necessary. Email addresses may also be used to inform you of any updates to our Terms of Service and/or Privacy Policy, security alerts, launch of new features, and any other events that may be of interest to you.

    (B) when you use our Services

    Once you have signed up, you are able to customize your profile through the use of display pictures and bio. Meem may collect this information as it helps it understand its users and, in time, enhance their individual experiences and suggest relevant content. Additionally, when you begin to use our Services, We may collect different types of information that you would input. For example, when engaging with other users in public conversations, such public messages may be collected to detect trends and allow Us to build a better experience for our users. As for direct messages, to protect the privacy of our users, Meem uses RSA and AES technologies to ensure that nobody except the user has access to his/her direct messages. Any direct message that you send to our servers remains there in encrypted form and cannot be viewed by anybody including Meem.

    (C) when you get in touch with Us

    If you’re faced with a problem, have any unanswered questions, want to share a suggestion or would like to get in touch with Us for any reason, our support team would always be happy to hear from you. Communications that take place between you and Meem in this regard are stored to enable Meem to respond to you promptly and assist you through all means possible.

  • 2. Information you provide Us about others

    Meem’s referral feature allows users to share the App with family and friends. If you permit Meem to access the contact lists on your mobile device by granting it permission through the system used by your mobile platform, Meem may access and store names and contact information from your contact lists to facilitate its referral feature, to understand the frequency of referrals and for other purposes described in this Privacy Policy.

  • 3. Information about your online activities

    This covers different aspects of your activities on our Services, and would include information such as the times at which you are online, the times at which you update your bio, timestamps of your messages and the users you interact with. Collecting and analyzing information about users’ online activities allows Us to detect trends and reach key metrics, which enable it to build a better understanding of its users, and improve and customize its Services to what would best suit each user individually.

  • 4. Information about your usage experience

    Meem collects different information about your experience using the Services, this may include diagnostics, performance logs and any crash reports. The aforementioned information helps Meem fix technical issues and provide you with a smooth experience while using our Services.

  • 5. Information about your location and information used to establish your location

    Meem collects some information to identify your location based such as GPS, Bluetooth signals, nearby cell towers and wifi networks as well as IP addresses. This information allows Meem to determine your location, and consequently be able to offer you content that relates to the same. The same information may be used for diagnostics and troubleshooting purposes in the case of you facing any technical difficulties with a location necessary feature. Lastly, your location may also be used to promote contests, special offers, and other events that relate to your surroundings.

  • 6. Information about your device and connection

    This covers information pertaining to the device that you use to access the Services, as well as the connection you are using to gain access to it. This may include information such as your operating system and version, device language and device timezone. Such information is useful to Meem as it allows Us to provide you with a smooth user experience.

  • 7. Information collected by third-party service providers

    To allow Us to provide our Services and continuously improve them, We work with Firebase, a Google cloud platform, as our backend server. From the variety of services that Firebase offers, Meem uses the following features:

    Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Realtime Database, Firebase Hosting, Cloud Functions for Firebase (Beta), Cloud Firestore (Beta), Cloud Storage for Firebase, Firebase Authentication, Firebase Test Lab, Firebase Crash Reporting, Firebase Performance Monitoring, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Firebase Dynamic Links, Firebase Remote Config, Firebase A/B Testing (Beta), Firebase Predictions (Beta), ML Kit for Firebase (beta), FireBase App Indexing, Crashlytics.

    Some of the above features may collect and process different types of information relating to the users of our Services. However, none of the above features processes personally identifying information, such as full name, email addresses or passwords. Additionally, Meem does not combine any personally identifying information that it may have independently collected with the information collected through the use of Firebase.

    At the heart of Firebase is Google Analytics for Firebase, an analytics solution that integrates across Firebase’s other features. In using Google Analytics for Firebase, We particularly implement the following features: Analytics intelligence, reporting, data analysis and visualization, data collection and management, Data activation.     Meem may also use other third-party service providers in the future to monitor and analyze the use of its Services.

How do We use cookies and other similar technologies?

We use cookies in our Services to be able to keep you logged into our App. We also use registration tokens that are associated with users’ devices, to enable proper functioning of our Services features including the delivery of notifications.

Do We use your information for advertising purposes?

The information collected from you may be used in the future for advertising purposes. In this regard, Non-personally identifying, de-identified and aggregate information may be shared with a third party advertising partner to allow Meem to provide you with ads that are likely to speak to you, as well as to measure their effectiveness.

How do We use cookies and other similar technologies?

  • Profile information: Your username, profile photo, bio is public by default, and may, hence, be accessed by search engines and APIs.
  • Direct messages: direct messages are fully encrypted and can only be viewed by the sender and receiver.
  • Public messages: public messages can only be viewed by Meem and other participants in the same conversation.
  • Non-personally identifying, de-identified and aggregate information: Meem may share non-personally identifying, de-identified and aggregate information about its users with third parties for research, marketing, advertising, or any other similar purposes.
  • Legal requests: When faced with legal requests such as public prosecution warrants and court orders, Meem will comply and hand over your requested information if it believes in good faith that it is required to do so. Meem will try to give you prior notice of the same if possible.
  • Protection of Meem and others: Where Meem believes that disclosing any of the information it collects from its users is necessary to: (a) protect the safety, rights or property of Meem, its users or others, (b) investigate, prevent, or take action with regards to potential or current violations of its Terms of Service, or (c) to address any data security and privacy concerns, it may disclose such information to such ends.
  • Change of ownership: Where Meem is involved in a merger, acquisition, asset sale, reorganization, bankruptcy or liquidation, users’ personal data may be transferred as a part of such transactions.
